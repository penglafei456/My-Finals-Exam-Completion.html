How Did I Get Here?

You know when an opportunity arises to you? Something so unbelievable to you that you start to question its own legitimate? If I stepped back and ran away from it, I wouldn't be holding a plane ticket to Taiwan. Such an experience before was not easy, such as gathering the needed requirements that were proven difficult to attain. But even so, I want to thank the support I've gotten, tho skeptical and worried, my family and friends supported me before I waved my last goodbye till I meet them again.

Course Conflict & Equal Confusion

The course chosen wasn't exactly the one I wanted to pursue in the future. Although technology is not a foreign concept to me, the studies that involved tech, mainly tackling software features were not something I had prior knowledge of. Not only that but as a foreigner in a country that barely spoke english was a huge struggle to me. Linguistic and Technology Issues aside, living conditions and new environment took me a while to adjust.

Worthwhile Experiences

Even in those sleepless nights, tired mornings, and never-ending work and school responsibilities, you can never go wrong with going out for relaxation trips or hanging out with friends. In my free time, I would love to play games, especially with my dear friends from here or back at my home, Philippines. I also travel around Kaoshiung to buy merchandise of my favorite KPOP groups or Anime. These things make the busy life more bareable than anything

Future Expectations & Hopeful Beginnings

Even in one year, this is just yet the beginning of something much bigger and brighter. Whatever the future brings for me, I will be ready to take flight and fight. I hope that in the near days coming, I do hope that I don't give up on the goal I've scarred on my skin since then.
